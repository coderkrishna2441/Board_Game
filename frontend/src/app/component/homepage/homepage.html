About Us

Our board game café is designed to create a welcoming space where people can disconnect from digital distractions and enjoy meaningful time together. Inspired by a **1990s retro atmosphere combined with a modern café aesthetic**, the environment blends nostalgic design elements with a comfortable and stylish setting. Instead of Wi-Fi and technology, the focus is on **traditional entertainment such as board games and card games**, encouraging interaction, teamwork, and friendly competition among friends and family. With relaxing background music, quality refreshments, and a wide selection of games, the café provides a unique social experience where visitors can unwind, engage, and create memorable moments through face-to-face connection. 🎲☕
